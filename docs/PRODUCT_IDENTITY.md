Conferly is being repositioned from "video conferencing platform" to "collaborative communication platform" — emphasizing multilingual collaboration, inclusion, accessibility, and meaningful conversations over technical infrastructure.

## PART 2: New Product Positioning

### Core Identity

> **Conferly** — A space where teams, schools, and communities communicate across language barriers. No downloads. No complexity. Just conversation.

### Value Proposition

- **For Teams**: Work together regardless of language
- **For Schools**: Parents join in their own language
- **For Communities**: Everyone participates
- **For Business**: Coordination without friction

### Emotional Framework

Instead of "video conferencing," think:
- "Gathering"
- "Conversation"
- "Bringing people together"
- "Shared understanding"
- "Participation"
